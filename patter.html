Welcome to Patter

Type a name for your chat room and click the create button. Then app.net members can join by visiting your unique URL.

Remember, Patter makes public chat rooms. Any app.net member can join using the URL. While your posts won't appear in your main timeline, anyone who cares to can find out what you write here.
