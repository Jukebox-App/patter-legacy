Welcome to Patter

Patter is an app.net client for real time chatting off your main stream.

You can create your own chat rooms and be directed to a unique URL. Any app.net member can join by visiting that URL.

Your room is actually an app.net post, and every chat message is a reply to that post. These posts are not visible on your main timeline, but they are still publicly available.
